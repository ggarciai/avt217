Instructions

  1. Open Adobe XD
  2. Create a canvas at W1200px by H800px
  3. Create a rectangle that is W600px by H400px and place it flushed to the left corner. Color should be Hex#E2AE38
  4. Create a new shape that is W600px by H200px and place it flushed to the right corner. Then bring it down by 200px. Color should be Hex#981010
  5. Create a new shape that is W600px by H400px and center it on the canvas then push it down to be flushed to the bottom of the canvas just below the other two shapes. Color should be #485065
  6. Sit back and enjoy your masterpiece
